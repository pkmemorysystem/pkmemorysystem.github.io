How to handle No Pronouns

By default, the members who do not have pronouns set up for them will display "No Pronouns" in that field, but if the "Do not show" option is selected, there will be a black space instead.
It will still take up space (and be rendered as a space) for future editing.

Divider Handling

A divider is a PluralKit member created to be a divider when switching, and they usually indicate who is co-conscious. With this option, they won't appear in the member list, but will appear in the fronters list and the add to front list.
A divider member has their name start and end with 3 backticks (```), so that when the user looks at the front history with PluralKit, the divider member is clearly visible as a divider.
